Learning Better Together

SP brings everyone in the education community together to help learners succeed.
We create technology, content and platforms that connect teachers, students
and parents to each other and help all learners discover their passions and
improve their skills.

Our Values

We believe edtech has the power to improve education and enrich the lives of everyone
in the education community, no matter where they might live, work, or play.
We put the needs of teachers and learners first in everything we build and do.

Our Impact

Since launching in 2008, Edmodo has helped over 100 million users learn
better together in hundreds of thousands of schools, all over the world.
And with over 700 million resources shared, teachers, students and parents
can find anything they need to help inspire the next generation of doers,
makers, and leaders.
